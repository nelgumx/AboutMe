About

01 / Profile

My engineering career began in the power generation industry, where I gained hands-on experience working with complex plant systems and equipment. I started as a Field Operator at FDC Misamis Power Corporation, performing equipment inspections, supporting preventive maintenance, and assisting during plant startups and shutdowns — building a strong foundation in operations, troubleshooting, and safety practices.

I progressed to Control Room Engineer, monitoring and controlling plant operations using DCS and SCADA systems, working closely with field and maintenance teams to maintain stable, efficient performance.

Recognizing my growing technical and analytical capabilities, I was promoted to Plant Performance Engineer — conducting performance testing based on ASME standards, analyzing operational data, and performing root cause analyses to reduce equipment downtime. I also developed Power BI dashboards to monitor KPIs, fuel consumption, and maintenance performance.

After more than eight years building my engineering career in the Philippines, I moved to the UAE to expand my professional experience. I currently work as a Maintenance Coordinator at Hertz – Al-Futtaim, coordinating maintenance activities, reviewing repair quotations, and streamlining workflows between workshop teams and vendors.

My goal is continuous career growth while contributing meaningful value to every organization I work with.

03 / Dashboards & tools
Power BI

Plant KPI Monitoring Dashboard

Power BI

Fuel Consumption & Heat Rate Dashboard

Visualizes daily and monthly fuel consumption and heat rate trends, helping engineers understand conversion efficiency and spot unusual changes early.

Power BI · Plant Operations

Plant Performance Dashboard

A centralized view of key operational data from the power generation system — power generation, fuel consumption, efficiency, and system conditions — organized across boiler performance, turbine operation, fuel and air flows, emissions, and water systems. Charts, performance tables, and simplified system diagrams illustrate how energy moves through the plant, from combustion to electricity generation.

Power BI · Fuel Handling

Fuel Inventory Dashboard

Tracks coal movement from incoming vessels to storage yards and plant units, with stock levels, stacking and reclaim volumes, blending distribution, and fuel quality metrics like heating value, moisture, ash, and sulfur content.

Power BI · Fleet Maintenance

Garage Turnaround Time Dashboard

Real-time monitoring of vehicle maintenance across multiple external garages — repair ageing, release forecasts, a preventive Service Due schedule by make and model, and a Vehicle Search feature for fast access to fleet details.
