01 — About

Engineering Operations. Driven by Data.

I am a Mechanical Engineer with over 8 years of experience in the power generation industry. My career started in a coal-fired thermal power plant, where I worked in operations, monitoring boilers, turbines, and auxiliary systems through DCS/SCADA. I handled unit start-ups and shutdowns, plant monitoring, and maintenance activities to ensure safe and reliable operations.

I later transitioned into Plant Performance Engineering, where I analyzed plant efficiency, conducted ASME PTC performance tests, monitored heat rate and fuel consumption, and developed Power BI dashboards to support data-driven decisions.

During this role, I discovered my passion for data analytics. I enjoyed finding patterns in data, solving problems through analysis, and turning information into meaningful insights. Since then, I have continued developing my skills in Power BI, SQL, Python, Excel, and data visualization.

Today, I work in the UAE coordinating fleet maintenance activities, managing daily maintenance tasks, reviewing repair quotations, and building dashboards that improve planning and decision-making. My engineering experience has strengthened my analytical and problem-solving skills, while my interest in data continues to drive my growth.

My goal is to continuously learn new skills, expand my knowledge, and apply what I learn to create value wherever I work. I believe continuous learning helps improve processes, solve challenges, and make a meaningful impact in every organization.
